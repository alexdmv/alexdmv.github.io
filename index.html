An Important Note Regarding COVID-19

To Our Valued Students,

Thank you for being a valuable member of Alex Driving School community. As the coronavirus (COVID-19) situation evolves, our highest priority remains the health, safety and well-being of our students. We are taking precautionary measures in accordance with guidelines established by the Center for Disease Control (CDC) and local health authorities to protect the health of our community. These include: We disinfect the car after each session. We are cleaning our car with appropriate cleaners, providing hand sanitizers and increasing sanitization of high-touch surfaces.

Staying informed: We are monitoring all CDC communications and staying safe. Confirming student’s health: We are asking any student who is not feeling well to please stay home and to seek medical attention. These additional layers of action are precautionary and are designed to help ensure the peace of mind you expect from Alex Driving School.

We are committed to safeguarding your well-being and to make sound decisions based on the most up-to-date guidelines established by the CDC and local health authorities. As of now, Alex Driving School remains open. However, we understand that whether or not to participate in a session or class is a personal choice so if you would like to cancel an appointment, we are happy to help you reschedule or will provide you credit towards future use.

Once again, thank you for being a part of Alex Driving School. We look forward to seeing you again soon.
